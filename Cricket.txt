Cricket

Cricket is one of the most popular sports in the world, especially in countries like India, Australia, England, and Pakistan. It is played between two teams of eleven players each on a circular or oval-shaped field.

The game is played with a bat and ball. One team bats to score runs, while the other team bowls and fields to restrict runs and dismiss players. The central part of the field is called the pitch, where most of the action takes place.

There are three main formats of cricket:
1. Test Cricket – Played over five days.
2. One Day Internationals (ODIs) – 50 overs per side.
3. T20 Cricket – 20 overs per side, fast-paced and popular.

Cricket requires a combination of skills such as batting, bowling, and fielding. Famous tournaments include the ICC Cricket World Cup and leagues like the Indian Premier League (IPL).

The sport is known for its strategy, teamwork, and excitement, making it loved by millions of fans worldwide.
